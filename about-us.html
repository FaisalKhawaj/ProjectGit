Welcome to our college with Love , Compassion and Faith

Our humble institute, initiated its journey in the year 2000 in a rented building on Karakuram Highway Mansehra.Over the short period of time institute now owns 5 campuses with state of the art infrastructure offering variety of programs.This college situated in the beautiful place in Mansehra and now became an exemplary and renowned institute of the country because of its devotion to excellence in the realm of education.
We aim to accomplish total quality in our all pursuits and to sustain the positive effort to achieve the truth by exploring the deep realms of knwoledge.Working with the belief that "Devotion is yours,Dedications is ours & Decison is with Allah"
Peace College is a very special place to be ,a humble dream to spread the knowledge to its highest and to enhance the learning capabilities of our youth. Here teachers,parents and students work exceptionally well together to create a warm ,vibrant and friendly atmosphere. As educationists and guides of future generation we are creating awareness in the students to adopt a living which should align with thier cause to come forward, to be a warrior of light and share their knowedge to enlighten the whole society.world. Education is the basic weapon of progessin any human society ,which not only equps is for material war but also exuberates eternal realm of the human soul.Successful & gleaming future of education lies in good and proficient institutions.The need for a high quality institution in Mansehra was very vital,because Abbottabad ,which is known for its nationally reputed instituesis far away from the reach of comon people of Mansehra and nearby villages.Therefore, those students who want to equip themselves with quality education have to travel daily or otherwise live in hostel.
The college has well equipped dispensary with two bed,medical inspection room; the medical officer and one assistant is available to provide medical care to students. The school has adequate arrangements to treat ailments of not to serious of nature like minor cuts,bruises and vomiting etc.
A qualified librarian is looking after the college library. the libray is spacious,fully furnished and proplerly ventilated . it houses more than 3,000 books on variety of academic and non acadmeic topics for all age groups.Daiy newspaper in English and Urdu,local and foreign magazines and periodicals are also subscribed.A weekly library lesson is fixed for every class to broaden students intellectual horizon and toformulate their reading habits..The institute's library isnamed as "Prof.DR.Ihsan Ali Library" to recognize and acknowledge the effortt and hardwork of Prof.Dr.Ihsan Ali(Vice Chancellor Hazara University ) for promoting higher education.
There are well equipped laboratories for Physics ,Chemistry and Biology to support teaching of science curriculum.Weekly practical session takes place according to the subjects in these laboratories.
